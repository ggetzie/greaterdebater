To create a bookmarklet for quickly submitting topics from your iPhone, follow the steps below:

  1. Copy this text:

    javascript:location.href='http://greaterdebater.com/submit?url='+encodeURIComponent(location.href)+'&title='+encodeURIComponent(document.title)

  2. Open your bookmarks menu and create a new bookmark.
  3. Paste the text into the bookmark's address field. Enter the title as "Submit to GreaterDebater" (or whatever you wish to call it)
  4. Save the bookmark.

That's all there is to it! Now, when viewing an article on your iPhone that you'd like to submit to GreaterDebater, simply visit that saved bookmark and you'll be redirected to the submit page with the title and url fields already filled out.
