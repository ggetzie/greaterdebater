Tips for submitting to GreaterDebater

  1. The goal of GreaterDebater is to encourage interesting discussions. To this end, topics are ranked according to the amount of comments they generate. To help your topic be more visible, be sure to include a paragraph or two describing your position on the topic you're submitting.
  2. Don't submit spam. Not only is it a vile profession, but you are wasting your time. It will never appear on the site. Surely, you can think of something better to do with your life.
  3. Do feel free to submit your own material if you believe it would be of interest to our users. If you've written an interesting blog post that you think might stir up some exciting controversy, let's have it.
  4. Submit faster with the GreaterDebater bookmarklet. Simply drag the link below to your browser's toolbar, then click on that bookmark when you're on a page you wish to submit.

    GreaterDebater
